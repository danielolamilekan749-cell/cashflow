# CashFlow AI Dashboard UI/UX Design Prompt

Design a world-class modern SaaS dashboard called **CashFlow AI**, an AI-powered business intelligence and customer retention platform built as an integration and plugin for Nomba merchants.

The goal is to create a dashboard that feels premium, intelligent, futuristic, and highly professional while remaining simple enough for everyday business owners to use.

This should not look like traditional accounting software. It should feel like the future of business management powered by AI.

---

## Design Philosophy

The entire experience should communicate:

* Simplicity
* Intelligence
* Trust
* Business Growth
* Automation
* Premium Technology

The dashboard should feel like a combination of:

* Stripe
* Linear
* Notion
* Apple
* Modern fintech products

Avoid clutter.

Avoid large bulky cards.

Avoid old-fashioned enterprise software layouts.

Everything should feel clean, light, spacious, and modern.

---

# Color System

Primary Color:

* Soft Premium Yellow (#F5C84C)

Secondary Colors:

* White (#FFFFFF)
* Off White (#FAFAFA)

Accent Colors:

* Deep Black (#111111)
* Charcoal (#2A2A2A)

Success:

* Soft Green

Warning:

* Soft Orange

Danger:

* Soft Red

Use yellow only as a highlight color.

Do not overuse yellow.

The dashboard should remain mostly white with subtle yellow accents.
